Kara Liu

I am a fourth year Computer Science major at UC Berkeley and an undergraduate researcher at Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (BAIR) Laboratory, where I work on long-horizon visual planning and representation learning under Pieter Abbeel and Aviv Tamar.

At Google I've worked on Lens Blur, HDR+, Jump, Portrait Mode, and Glass. I did my PhD at UC Berkeley, where I was advised by Jitendra Malik and funded by the NSF GRFP. I did my bachelors at the University of Toronto.
